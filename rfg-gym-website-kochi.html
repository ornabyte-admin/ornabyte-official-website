Project Overview

We designed and developed a modern, multi-page website for RFG Fitness Studio — helping them turn curious visitors into active members. With WhatsApp integration, a lead-focused enquiry form, and mobile-optimized design, the site is as active and functional as the studio itself.

The site includes pages for programs, trainers, and FAQs, all designed with clean UX and responsive layouts. This gives RFG Fitness a strong online presence that mirrors the energy of their physical space.

  • Multi-page HTML, CSS, JS implementation
  • WhatsApp integration for real-time chat
  • Custom enquiry form to generate leads
  • Responsive and performance-optimized design

From Lead to Lift — A Fitness Site That Works

RFG Fitness didn’t just want a website — they needed a platform to generate leads and engage clients. Our work turned their goals into reality with design that lifts conversions and performance equally.

We started by learning about the studio's offerings, goals, and user needs — laying the foundation for a goal-driven structure.

We designed a bold, clean layout that balances energy with ease — using strong colors, visual hierarchy, and device-friendly structure.

We built the site with clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — ensuring performance and compatibility. The WhatsApp API and form handlers were custom coded for speed and ease.

After launch, RFG saw increased enquiries via form and WhatsApp, with customers complimenting the ease of access and fast performance.
