Project Overview

One-Page Design & Development

SugaryPie is a local bakery that wanted to go digital — without the fuss. We built a single-page website that showcased their full product line with rich visuals and WhatsApp-based ordering, helping customers discover, crave, and buy their favorite treats instantly.

The site is responsive, fast-loading, and optimized for mobile-first browsing — so that a sweet craving can turn into a real order with just one tap. From cakes to cookies, SugaryPie now has a clean digital storefront that works just as beautifully as it looks.

  • One-page HTML, CSS, JS implementation
  • WhatsApp integrated for direct customer orders
  • Fully mobile-optimized layout for quick browsing
  • Modern design tailored to bakery brand feel

From Shelf to Screen — in One Page

SugaryPie didn’t need a complex site — they needed visibility, charm, and click-to-order functionality. This project proves that simplicity, when paired with great design and tech, can transform how small businesses grow online.

Web Design Process

We sat with the client to understand their product list, customer types, and pain points — identifying a WhatsApp ordering flow that matched their existing behavior.

We created a clean layout with visual hierarchy that makes every product pop — using color and space to match the warm feel of a neighborhood bakery.

Built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS, the site was optimized for fast loading and low-maintenance. WhatsApp links were added to each item for direct customer communication.

The client loved the result — and more importantly, customers now send direct orders regularly, thanks to the simple interface and call-to-action layout.
