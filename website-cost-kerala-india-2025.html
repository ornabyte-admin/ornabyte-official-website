How Much Does a Website Cost in Kerala? (2025 Pricing Guide)

If you're a business owner, startup founder, or entrepreneur in Kerala, this 2025 pricing guide will give you clarity on website costs — and how to make the most of your investment.

Author
Ornabyte
Published
July 10, 2025

Planning to launch your business online in Kerala? One of the first questions you’ll have is, "How much will a website actually cost me?" The truth is, pricing can range from ₹5,000 for a basic site to ₹1,50,000+ for a full custom build — depending on features, goals, and who you hire.

In this 2025 guide, we break down real examples, pricing factors, and smart tips for startups and small businesses in Kerala looking to build a professional website without getting ripped off.

Factors That Affect Website Cost in Kerala

  • Type of Website: Single-page to full-stack builds
  • CMS vs. Custom Build: WordPress is cheaper ; custom is scalable
  • SEO & Content Strategy: SEO adds cost but multiplies ROI
  • Design Style: Template vs. custom design impacts cost
  • Who Builds It: Freelancers vs Agencies vs Niche studios

Real-World Website Pricing Examples (Kerala, 2025)

Type Features Estimated Cost
Basic Portfolio 1 page, contact form ₹7,000 – ₹10,000
Business Website 5–7 pages, SEO, mobile-friendly ₹18,000 – ₹30,000
E-commerce 10+ pages, cart, payment gateway ₹45,000 – ₹90,000
Custom App Backend + frontend (Django/React) ₹1,20,000+

Based on quotes from Kerala-based developers and agencies.
